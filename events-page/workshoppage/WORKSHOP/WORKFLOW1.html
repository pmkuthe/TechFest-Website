EVENT DATE : 14TH & 15TH FEBRUARY 2019
EVENT VENUE : GOVERNMENT COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING , NAGPUR.
EVENT TIME: 10:30AM – 4:30PM

Description:
This is the 2nd iteration of the hugely successful workshop that was conducted last year . This Workshop aims to teach a student different aspects of photography and videography in a very simple and interactive way . The Trainer Mr. Rajdeep Wasekar is a Freelancer by profession with many international clients .

  Course Contents : In the Workshop , the following things would be taught –
Photography Compositions
Skin Retouching
Architecture Retouching
Product Retouching
Food Photographs Retouching
The Theory of Colour Science
Colour Grading
3D Tracking
Rotoscoping Introduction
Compositing Introduction
