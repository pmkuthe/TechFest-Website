STARGAZE

Event Date:- 14th And 15th Feb, 2019
Event Venue- Govt. College Of Engineering, Nagpur
Event Time- 14th Feb- 12pm To 5pm 15th Feb- 12am To 3pm Observation Session: 14th Feb after 06:30 Pm



DESCRIPTION

The event mainly focuses on the basic concepts of astronomy. The whole event is divided into four parts
Day 1:-1) Basic concepts:- Students will be explain with the concepts of astronomy which are required for basic research purposes and some misconceptions which are needed to be cleared

2) Experiments:- Total of 3 experiments will be shown to the students to clear the common misconceptions of the students

3) Introduction to telescope:- Student should be taught how telescope works and hands-on-experience will be provided to the students on various aspects related to telescope such as alignment, how to track objects etc. Observation session:- An observation session will be conducted for the participating contestants this event will take place on 14th after 06:30 pm* (*This event is subject to weather conditions)

Day 2:- 4) Software handling: Students will be taught how to use the basic data handling and image processing software related to astronomy.

Software used: 1) SAO Ds9 (A Harvard developed software) 2) Stellerium 3) WorldWide Telescope Note:- Students are requested to bring their own laptops. Refreshments will be provided on the first day of the workshop
