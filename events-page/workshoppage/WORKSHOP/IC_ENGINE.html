IC ENGINE

Event Date : 14th & 15th February 2019
Event Venue : Auditorium ( Room no. 2) , Government College Of Engineering , Nagpur.
Event Time:



Description: This is a 2-day workshop on Advanced Automotive Mechanics . All the basics and the working principles of an IC Engine would be explained . Hands-on would be done on a Hyundai Car Engine and a Bike Engine. The Trainer Mr. Ashish Jha is an ex-Mercedes-Benz employee who used to do the Diagnosis of Mercedes-Benz Cars and the Troubleshooting of Faults.

 Course Content for Advanced Automotive Mechanics Session-1 Basic Information & Automotive Engine Systems Introduction Session -1 History and Indian Automotive Industry Industry’s Demand Automotive Engine Fuels Engine Fuel & Exhaust Systems Superchargers Turbochargers Electronic-Fuel-Injection Systems Diesel Fuel-Injection Systems Engine Lubricating Systems Engine Cooling Systems


Session-2 Engine Performance and Driveability Automotive Emission-Control Systems Servicing Emission-Control Systems Engine Test Equipment Tuneup Engine Performance Drive ability Diagnosis


Session-3 Automotive Drive Trains Automotive Clutches: Operation & Service Manual Transmissions & Transaxles Driveshaft, Universal Joints, Differentials, Drive axles Four-Wheel Drive, Transfer Cases Viscous Couplings Automatic Transmissions Transaxles Session-3 Automotive Chassis Automotive Suspension Systems Automotive Steering Systems Wheel-Alignment, Steering, Suspension Automotive Brakes Antilock Braking System Electronic Stability Program Antilock Braking, Traction Control, Brake Diagnosis Tires & Wheels : Construction Automotive Heating & Air Conditioning Ventilation, Heating, Air Conditioning

Session-4 Quiz Competition
