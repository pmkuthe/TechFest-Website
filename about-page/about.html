"ADHYAAYA" is the technical symposium of GOVERNMENT COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING, NAGPUR. A forum where people share their ideas and innovate; Adhyaaya is a place to nurture the talents of budding technocrats.lt provides a pathway to students for finding their real interests and can work upon it. ADHYAAYA features a variety of events pertaining to different branches . With a plethora of events and seminars by great luminaries to cater students of various frameworks. ADHYAAYA has set the benchmark of excellence. Since its inception from 2018, it has been improving and is taking a leap towards becoming a great platform for sophomore's from Nagpur and all over Maharashtra. It has witnessed participation of students from prominent states such as Andhra Pradesh, Chattisgarh, Gujarat. Karnataka, Punjab and Telangana. Just in a year, ADHYAAYA is carving a niche as one of the promising technical symposiums in Nagpur.
